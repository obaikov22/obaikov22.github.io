About
A bit about me

I'm Oleg, 28, living in London since 2022. By day I work as a robotics operator at Atlas FM, managing a fleet of autonomous cleaning robots at Bloomberg's offices.

Mobile development started as a way to solve my own problems at work — I needed better tools to manage my robots, so I built them myself. That first app turned into a real project, and now I'm hooked.

I enjoy the process of turning a real problem into a clean, working solution. I'm available for freelance Android projects — especially tools and utilities that make people's work lives easier.
